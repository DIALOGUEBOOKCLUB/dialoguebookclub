Sula
by Toni Morrison
In this short story collection by the London based author Vida Adamczewski, each pourous narrative soaks into one another. Characters, places and problems coalesce into an intruiging pond-like form which spawns esoteric yet quietly tenacious voices and curiously anatomical imagery. Creep to this pond quietly and move slowly. Surrender your body to its lyrical language.
